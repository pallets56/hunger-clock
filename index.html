In 2021, Elon Musk told the UN’s World Food Programme that if they gave him a plan to solve world hunger for $6 billion, he’d pay.

So they gave him a plan.

Fully costed. $0.43 a meal, 42 million people, 43 countries.

He never paid.

Since then he’s made over $700 billion and become the world’s first trillionaire.

He could fund that plan 166 times over.

Instead he chooses not to.
